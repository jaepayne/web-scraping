3.0 out of 5 starsI like it, good value for 3-pack. But it's not what you think it is (doesn't really "disinfect")
By Mad Max TOP 500 REVIEWERVINE VOICE on February 22, 2013
Size: 240 countPackage Type: Standard Packaging Vine Customer Review of Free Product ( What's this? )
This 3-pack is a good value, and we use them for the "germ centers" in our house (litter box, bathroom, kitchen sink, door knobs, etc).

These are particularly good for cleaning things that often get forgotten (like the tank & flush handle on the toilet, the outside of the litter box, etc).

However, read the small print! This product doesn't ~really~ disinfect like you think it does. It was a real disappointment to discover this.

According to the packaging (and the Lysol web site), you have to soak the surface &/or scrub for 10 minutes (YES... TEN MINUTES) or else it's not really "disinfected" against all those things listed (flu virus, bacteria, etc). Alternatively, if you scrub &/or let moisture sit for a minimum of 30 seconds, it's "sanitized" (not disinfected). I suppose this depends a bit on weather and humidity - how quickly the damp chemicals sit before evaporating.

So for many of us, if you just wipe off the surface (like 99.99% of us probably do), then Lysol says all you're doing is cleaning off dust & surface dirt. This is troubling - in other words, it's no more effective than using a Bounty + soap & water.

This is why I only use the Lysol wipes for the germ centers of the house - there's no reason to spread chemicals all over the house if it's not providing a benefit.

For daily cleaning of most household surfaces, we use a spray bottle of 7th Generation (or sometimes, just 50/50 white vinegar & water), and a cloth or paper towel.

One other heads-up - the bottle leaks & the wipes dry out if stored (or shipped) on their side.
